Hello and Welcome!

I am Gustavo Diaz and I made this website to help jump-start people who want to learn Japanese.

When I first started teaching myself Japanese, I encountered many obstacles and roadblocks that made learning Japanese seem like an impossible task.

I found many of the grammar points and translations in various textbooks to be confusing and inadequate. Phrases and sayings were thrown your way and they were not properly or throughly explained. More questions came up than were answered.

In this website I will give my explanation and understanding of Japanese that I have gathered from various textbooks and from my college courses. I will assume you the reader has virtaully no background in Japanese. I will aslo provide external resources that do a great job of explaining the content.
